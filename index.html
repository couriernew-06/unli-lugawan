What's special about this lugawan?

My chosen business is a “Lugawan”, the approach of this business is to provide unlimited lugaw for each customer.

I prepared various Internet of [Enchanted] Things concepts wherein the machines talk to one another together with a Business-to-Consumer type of E-commerce.

I have seen food businesses with this kind of strategy – unlimited offers, and I think that there’s a better way of implementing this idea by the use of Internet of things. The first IoT concept that I have for this business is an advance table setting where a machine is on the ceiling of the store.

What’s the purpose of this machine?

It is a machinery with lugaw inside it. Take note that the table is also an IoT concept. The table will be collecting data showing the status of the bowl and if it detected that the bowl is close to being empty, the table will send this data to the machine at the ceiling.

The machine will slightly go down and will prompt the customer if he/she wanted more of lugaw

With this concept, there is no need for assisting the customers every time they need their next batch of lugaw.

Chair and Tablet? How do they work?

The chair will record information about the condition of their customer – it will pass the information to the built-in tablet placed on each customer’s table

The information includes the health condition of their customer whether it is safe to have another bowl of lugaw or not.

It’ll also show the drinks and ingredients that makes him vulnerable.

The tablet will present a complete analytical data from time to time. (A customer has a choice to stop viewing the said data.)

What’s with this “Unli-Lugawan Enchanted Card”?

  • This will be connected to their accounts in the e-commerce site and application of “Unli-Lugawan”.
  • Their ID number will serve as their e-wallets whenever they order online or onsite.
  • The card itself can be used onsite: I designed this business to give the customers an option to automatically pay their bills by inserting the card on the table
  • As per the receipt, it will automatically be reflected on their account.
  • The tablet I mentioned earlier could also print a receipt if the customer wished to have it.

What’s “ENCHANTED” in this card?

The card disappears and reappear when it has detected that the card owner wants to eat and whenever “it” is needed specially when depositing money in it.

The next enchanted thing about this card is that it can elevate itself and could go anywhere in front the owner if he uttered words stating that he wants to see the card
